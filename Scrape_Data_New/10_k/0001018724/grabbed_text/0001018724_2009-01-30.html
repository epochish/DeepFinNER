INVESTMENTS

THE INITIAL CARRYING COST OF OUR INVESTMENTS IS THE PRICE WE PAID. ALL OTHER EQUITY INVESTMENTS CONSIST OF INVESTMENTS FOR WHICH WE DO NOT HAVE THE ABILITY TO EXERCISE SIGNIFICANT INFLUENCE. UNDER THE COST METHOD OF ACCOUNTING, INVESTMENTS IN PRIVATE COMPANIES ARE CARRIED AT COST AND ARE ADJUSTED ONLY FOR OTHER-THAN-TEMPORARY DECLINES IN FAIR VALUE, DISTRIBUTIONS OF EARNINGS, AND ADDITIONAL INVESTMENTS. FOR PUBLIC COMPANIES THAT HAVE READILY DETERMINABLE FAIR VALUES, WE CLASSIFY OUR EQUITY INVESTMENTS AS AVAILABLE-FOR-SALE AND, ACCORDINGLY, RECORD THESE INVESTMENTS AT THEIR FAIR VALUES WITH UNREALIZED GAINS AND LOSSES, NET OF TAX, INCLUDED IN “ACCUMULATED OTHER COMPREHENSIVE INCOME (LOSS),” A SEPARATE COMPONENT OF STOCKHOLDERS’ EQUITY.
