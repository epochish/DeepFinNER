INTEREST RATE RISK

&NBSP;&NBSP;&NBSP;&NBSP;&NBSP; THE PRIMARY OBJECTIVE OF OUR INVESTMENT ACTIVITIES IS TO PRESERVE PRINCIPAL WHILE AT THE SAME TIME MAXIMIZING YIELDS WITHOUT SIGNIFICANTLY INCREASING RISK.
